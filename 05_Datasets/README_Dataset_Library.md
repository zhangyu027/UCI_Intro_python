# UCI CDE Business Analytics Dataset Library

Original synthetic classroom-ready datasets for Python, pandas, visualization, and business analytics instruction.

Datasets:
1. Sales Analytics — sales, profit, region, category, customer segment.
2. Marketing Campaign — spend, impressions, clicks, conversions, revenue.
3. HR Employee Attrition — department, income, satisfaction, attrition.
4. Finance Credit Risk — income, loan amount, credit score, default.
5. Healthcare Diabetes Prediction — clinical risk factors and diabetes flag.
6. Manufacturing Predictive Maintenance — machine readings and failure flag.
7. Customer Churn Telecom — tenure, charges, contract, churn.
8. Supply Chain Shipments — warehouse, carrier, cost, delivery delay.

Suggested use:
- Mini challenges
- GroupBy practice
- Visualization labs
- Intro machine learning
- Capstone extensions
- Portfolio projects

Note: These are synthetic instructional datasets, not real company or patient data.
