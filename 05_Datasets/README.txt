# UCI CDE Business Analytics Dataset Library

Original synthetic classroom-ready datasets for Python, pandas, visualization, ML basics, and capstone extensions.

Datasets included:
1. Sales Analytics
2. Marketing Campaign Analytics
3. HR Employee Attrition
4. Finance Credit Risk / Loan Default
5. Healthcare Diabetes Prediction
6. Manufacturing Predictive Maintenance
7. Customer Churn
8. Supply Chain Shipments

These datasets are synthetic and designed for instruction. They are not real company, patient, employee, or customer records.

Suggested use:
- pandas practice
- groupby and aggregation
- visualization
- data cleaning
- machine learning introduction
- capstone extensions
